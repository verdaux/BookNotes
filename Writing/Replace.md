# Replace

- [ ] About -> of
- [ ] Very ->  'est of (rarest of events/very rare event)
- [ ] Are -> comma, (These are the men who stood/These, the men who stood)
- [ ] -able -> a matter of (it's disputable/ it's a matter of dispute)
- [ ] That he was -> him (I thought that he was wise/ I thought him wise)
- [ ] -ful -> a source of (her absence is regretful/her absence is a source of regret)
- [ ] With -> of (they were sitting in a blind with twigs/they were sitting in a bind of twigs)
