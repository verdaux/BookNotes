# Tyrants - Stalin

- [ ] Born in Gori, Georgia
- [ ] Birth name: Ioseb Dzhugashvili
- [ ] Takes the name - Stal (Russian) for steel
- [ ] Early 20s joined Bolsheviks - Marxist rebels looking to overthrow the Russian government
- [ ] Bolsheviks take power in 1917 - Lenin appointed Stalin a ministry
- [ ] 1922 - Stalin named general secretary of the communist party
- [ ] January 1924 - Lenin dies , Stalin takes charge of funeral
- [ ] 1929 - becomes dictator, controls and censors all information
- [ ] 1931 - famine
- [ ] Bribes Walter Duranty , new york times reporter in Moscow to sell leftist support and deflect from famine
